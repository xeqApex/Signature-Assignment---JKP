Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are your opening hours?

We are open from 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday to Friday, and from 10 AM to 4 PM on Saturday.

2. Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian options available on our menu.

3. Can I make a reservation?

Yes, you can make a reservation by calling us or booking online through our website.

4. Do you offer takeout?

Yes, we offer takeout services for all menu items.

5. Is parking available?

Yes, we have parking space available for our customers.

6. Do you have gluten-free options?

Yes, we provide gluten-free items; please ask our staff for recommendations.

7. Can I host an event at your restaurant?

Absolutely! Please contact us for more details on hosting private events.

8. What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, credit, and debit cards.
